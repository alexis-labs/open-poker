# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Open Poker to be a welcoming project for contributors with different backgrounds, skill levels, and perspectives. We commit to keeping project spaces respectful, constructive, and focused on collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and patient with other contributors.
- Give feedback that is specific and useful.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Respect maintainers' time and decisions.
- Credit work that is not yours.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors who do not follow this code of conduct.

If you need to report a problem, contact the repository maintainers through the project's preferred private channel once one is published.
